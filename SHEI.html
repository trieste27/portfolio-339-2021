SHEI Writing

I am a member of the Features Team at SHEI Magazine, the only officially recognized arts, culture, and fashion publication on campus. SHEI publishes monthly digital issues and biannual print issues, and my writing has been selected for several of them. Click any cover below to open the corresponding article in a new tab. And be sure to flip through the issue to see high-level student work!

As the magazine's Editorial Social Media Coordinator, I am responsible for collecting, organizing, scheduling, and posting content created by our Photo, Fashion, Features, Street Style and Video teams.

Click to visit @shei_magazine

I enjoy this role because I get to interact with members across the organization and bring professional-quality creative work to the attention of the Ann Arbor community.
