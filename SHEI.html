SHEI Writing

I am a member of the Features Team at SHEI Magazine, the only officially recognized arts, culture, and fashion publication on U-M's campus. SHEI publishes monthly digital issues and biannual print issues, and my writing has been selected for several. Click any cover on this page to open the corresponding article in a new tab. And be sure to flip through the rest of the issues to see high-level, interdiscplinary, creative student work.

I am also the magazine's Editorial Social Media Coordinator. This role involves collecting, organizing, scheduling, and posting content created by our Photo, Fashion, Features, Street Style and Video teams.

Click to visit @shei_magazine

I enjoy this role because I get to both interact with 100+ members across the organization and bring professional-quality creative work to the attention of the Ann Arbor community.
