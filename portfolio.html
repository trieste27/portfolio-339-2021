Welcome to my Writing Archive! My name is Hannah Triester and I am a junior studying User Experience Design at the University of Michigan's School of Information, with a minor in Public Policy at the Gerald R. Ford School of Public Policy.

I built this website as my final portfolio submission for SI 339: Web Design, Development, & Accessibility. This course provides practical and theoretical coverage to learning responsive, accessible front-end programming for Web Design. Topics covered include HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, and the principles of accessible design.

On this website you may access my writing for academic courses, SHEI Magazine, and my work at U-M's Center for Academic Innovation. Writing is how I contribute to the campus community and I am proud to present my work on a site I built myself.
